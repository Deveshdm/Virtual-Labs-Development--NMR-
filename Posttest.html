'Nuclear Magnetic Resosnance Spectrocopy and Evaulation of Simple 1H NMR Spectra of Select Organic Compounds'

Q1. An unknown molecule A has 4 signals in the 1H NMR spectrum. Which of the following corresponds to molecule A?
A    It has only one type of proton.
B    It has only two types of proton.
C    It has four types of protons.
D    It has six types of proton.
Hint: It has four types of protons.

Q2. What is the splitting pattern of the methylene protons in propane?
A    Triplet.
B    Quartet.
C    Doublet.
D    Septet.
Hint: Septet

Q3.How many different H environments for the molecule are shown? Question Image
A    4 Hydrogen environment, in (3.3.1.3) ratio
B    4 Hydrogen environment, in (3.1.6.3) ratio
C    3 Hydrogen environment, in (6.1.3) ratio
D    3 Hydrogen environment, in (3.6.3) ratio
Hint: 3 Hydrogen environment, in (6.1.3) ratio .

Q4. What is the multiplicity of the hydrogen bonded to oxygen in an NMR spectrum of pure ethanol?
A    Quintet
B    Triplet
C    Doublet
D    Quartet
Hint: Triplet.

Q5.Which of the following statements about the energy of an applied magnetic field is FALSE?
A    This energy can “flip” a proton from alignment with the field to alignment opposite the field.
B    Nuclei are “in resonance” when they absorb this energy.
C    This energy is proportional to the magnetic field strength.
D    This energy is supplied by radiation in the visible region of the spectrum.
Hint: This energy is supplied by radiation in the visible region of the spectrum.
